BAI CHOI

I created this project after researching the Vietnamese Folk Festival on the first day of the year 2024. I wondered that our culture is diverse but most of us-teenager are not interested in exploring and enquiring about it. So I came up with an idea to redesign these cards!

Through this project, I want to renew the appearance of the cards which are more attractive and easier to approach teenagers. The result is different from what I had done before and I found it gratifying even though this tiny project ate up more of my sleep time than I had anticipated.
